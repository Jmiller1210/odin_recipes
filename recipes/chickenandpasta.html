Chicken and Pasta

Description

This recipe from Jake's cookbook consists of marinated chicken cooked over the grill accompanied by a delicious parmesean pasta cooked with thick spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless chicken breast
  • Thyme
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Thick Spaghetti
  • Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. To make marinade for chicken, first add in a mixing bowl 1/4 cup olive oil, 1 tbsp thyme, salt, and black pepper, with a pinch of cayenne to taste.
  2. Pound chicken breast with a meat tenderizer and add to ziplock back with marinade. Allow to marinate for atleast 3 hours in refrigerator.
  3. Once chicken is sufficiently marinated, preheat grill for about 20 minutes until coals are red/white
  4. Once grill is pre-heated, cook chicken on grill for ~5 minutes each side, making sure internal temperature reaches 165F
  5. While chicken is cooking, start boiling water for pasta, ~8 cups until rolling boil.
  6. Add thick pasta to water and allow to cook for 10 minutes, or until pasta reaches desired tenderness.
  7. Once chicken is done cooking, remove from grill and allow to rest for 5 minutes before serving. While chicken is resting, strain water from pasta after sufficiently cooked and put onto a plate or bowl.
  8. Chicken can be cut up and mixed with pasta, or they can be eaten seperatly, but be sure to add parmesean cheese to the pasta for taste
  9. Enjoy!
