Meat 'n' Taters

Description

A personal favorite from Jake's recipe book, this meal consists of hamburger meat, cheese, all cooked together on the grill, with a hearty helping of barbecue sauce to top it off.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Hamburger Meat
  • 3 Idaho Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 1/4 Cup Light Brown Sugar
  • Sweet Baby Ray's Brown Sugar BBQ Sauce
  • One Bag of Bacon Bits

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes into bite size chunks
