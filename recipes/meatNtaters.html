Meat 'n' Taters

Description

A personal favorite from Jake's recipe book, this meal consists of hamburger meat, cheese, all cooked together on the grill, with a hearty helping of barbecue sauce to top it off.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Hamburger Meat
  • 3 Idaho Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 1/4 Cup Light Brown Sugar
  • Sweet Baby Ray's Brown Sugar BBQ Sauce
  • One Bag of Bacon Bits
  • 12oz Bag Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. add charcoals to grill, light grill and let warm for 20 minutes. proceed with potato preperation while grill warms
  2. Slice potatoes into bite size chunks
  3. Season potatoes to taste with salt and pepper
  4. Add potatoes into aluminum baking tray and mix in brown sugar
  5. Bake potatoes on grill after warming for 45 minutes
  6. Once potatoes are cooked thouroughly and can be easily pierced with a fork, remove from grill
  7. Add beef into large cooking pan on grill and season with salt and pepper
  8. Cook beef thouroughly on grill
  9. Mix beef in with potatoes and add a few more pinches of brown sugar to taste
  10. Once mixed, add in bacon bits and cheese
  11. Mix until cheese is well melted, then generously add barbecue sauce till mix is thick and creamy
  12. Allow to cool before serving
  13. Enjoy!
